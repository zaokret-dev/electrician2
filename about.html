Our Story

BUILT ON EXPERTISE,
DRIVEN BY INTEGRITY

SparkPro Electric was founded in 2007 by Tom Kowalski, a second-generation electrician who grew up watching his father wire homes across the South Side. His belief was simple: Chicago families deserve honest pricing, clean work, and an electrician who actually shows up on time.

What began as a solo operation out of a single van has grown into a team of 14 licensed electricians covering the entire Chicagoland area — from the North Shore to Naperville.
